How do I play?

Drag anywhere on the left or bottom of the screen to move — your weapons fire automatically at nearby enemies. Collect the gems dropped by defeated enemies to level up and choose new weapons and upgrades. Survive as long as you can.

What is Starfleet Command?

A second layer beyond survival runs: build a starship, crew it with drones, and claim planets on the star map — funded by Crystals earned in every run or purchased. Alliances and clan wars let you team up and raid rival fleets.

Gold vs. Crystals

Gold is earned in survival runs and spent in the survival-mode shop on permanent run upgrades. Crystals are a separate currency for the Starfleet Command layer (starship, drones, planets, the marketplace) — earned slowly through play or purchased via the Crystal Shop.

Restore purchases / ads

Rewarded ads are entirely optional and only ever grant a revive, bonus gold, or a temporary speed boost — declining an ad never blocks progress. Crystal purchases can be restored from the Crystal Shop screen.
