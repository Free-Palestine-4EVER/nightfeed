Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

NIGHTFEED ("the app") is a survivor-roguelite game with an optional online meta-layer (Starfleet Command: alliances, clan wars, and a Crystal marketplace). This policy explains exactly what data the app handles.

What the app stores locally on your device

Your Gold balance, permanent survival-mode upgrades, best survival time, and run count are stored locally using Apple's standard UserDefaults storage. This never leaves your device.

What the app stores in the cloud

If you use the Starfleet Command features (alliances, clan wars, the drone marketplace), the app creates an anonymous account (via Firebase Authentication) and stores your starship stats, Crystal balance, drone/planet ownership, and alliance membership in a cloud database so this progress can sync and other players can see it for PvP and trading. This account is anonymous — it is not linked to your name, email, or Apple ID unless you explicitly choose to link one in a future update.

Advertising

NIGHTFEED shows optional rewarded ads (to revive during a run, double your gold, or gain a temporary speed boost) and occasional interstitial ads, served through Google's AdMob. To show relevant ads, AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier if you grant permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt — you can decline this prompt and still play the full game. Google's own use of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy and their AdMob data disclosure.

In-app purchases

NIGHTFEED offers optional Crystal purchases via Apple's In-App Purchase system. Payment is processed entirely by Apple — we never see or store your payment details.

What we don't do

We do not sell your data. We do not have access to any data beyond what is described on this page, and we have no chat or user-generated content features.

Children's privacy

NIGHTFEED is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
