1. **Level-up card pull** — screen-record the level-up overlay popping in (the 3-card choice), timed so a rare gold-bordered ★ EVOLUTION ★ card is in the mix — it has its own idle glow/breathing animation that reads great in a slow-mo clip.
2. **Nightmaw mini-boss fight** — clip the Nightmaw spawning on its scheduled timer mid-swarm and going down, ideally with a screen full of regular Swarmlings/Bloodbats still on screen around it for scale.
3. **Weapon evolution first-fire** — capture the moment Fang Bolt maxes into Moonfang Barrage (or Reaper Whirl into Crimson Maelstrom) and fires for the first time — the payoff moment of a whole run's build.
4. **Late-run bullet-heaven chaos** — a dense clip late in an 18-minute run with Bat Swarm's homing shots and Ember Orbit's orbiters going off at once against a packed wave — the "screen full of stuff" shot this genre lives on.
5. **Command Deck pan** — screen-record the STARFLEET COMMAND home screen: the pulsing title, the ship with its orbiting drone badges (triangle/pentagon/diamond glyphs circling it), and the "ORBITING: [planet name]" readout.
6. **Starship Hangar upgrade screen** — pan across the hangar's upgrade rows (Hull Plating / Weapon Systems / Shield Array, each showing LVL x/10) and capture a stat ticking up on purchase.
7. **Star map planet hop** — cycle the nav chips across the unlock order (Emberwatch → Voidreach → Lunahaven → Crimsonforge → Ashenveil), showing the locked-planet lock icon and unlock cost for a not-yet-owned world.
8. **Gold shop between runs** — quick clip of spending gold on a permanent upgrade (health, damage, or the familiar) right after a death screen, showing the run-to-run progression loop.
